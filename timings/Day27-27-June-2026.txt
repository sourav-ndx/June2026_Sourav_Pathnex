Whay i learned , primarily what is SSL & TLS .

SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) is an older cryptographic protocol but it is now deprecated due to security vulnerabilities.
TLS (Transport Layer Security) is the modern, highly secure successor to SSL that encrypts internet traffic and authenticates server identities today.


TLS does a 3 way handshake , which means , The client and server perform a "handshake" to authenticate the server's identity using a digital certificate. They then agree on a temporary, unique session key to encrypt all data moving between them


Whats are self signed and CA signed certs , so self signed are the ones which we generate ouerselves , can be used in DEV and Non prod setup . CA certs are provided by Authorized Certificate Authorities like Digecert . Usually its paid and acts as a vlaidated entity for using in securing connections and handshake .

OPENSSSL commands are used to genrate certificates .
